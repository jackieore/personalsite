Who am I?

My name is Kaya Thomas. I am an undergraduate student at Dartmouth College studying computer science. I'm a programmer for Tiltfactor, game design lab focused on social change. I'm a part of the developing team for Metadata Games project, a digital gaming platform for gathering data on image artifacts, check out my contributions on my github. I've also designed a customized version of 2048 called Women in Tech Game.

My Journey into Tech

I've always loved technology and design, but growing up I thought this meant I should be en engineer. When I discover programming on Codecademy I knew it was what I was meant to do. The creative power of programming and computer science inspires me to bring my ideas to life through technology. I've learned Python, Java, HTML/CSS, and Javascript both in and outside the classroom. I believe it's very important to spread awareness about the growing technology field to communities that are underrepresented in tech careers. I have volunteered for Black Girls Code and hope to continue to spread my knowledge in hopes that the field of technology will become more diverse. Coding is such a fun hands-on skill that anyone can learn. It's a skill that can change your life.
