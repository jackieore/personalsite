About me

Mentorship, education and coding have been the focus of my work and inspiration. In high school I co-founded a STEM camp for girls. When I discovered coding in late 2013, I started as a tech mentor for Black Girls Code in 2014. I've also worked as a Section Leader and Teaching Assistant for Dartmouth's Computer Science department and a peer tutor for Tutor Clearinghouse. I have coded projects in various languages, but my primary languages are Objective C and Swift. When I'm not doing any of those things I love to read, run, dance, or write.
